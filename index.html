EduMeasure OBE Application

EduMeasure's OBE Application addresses one of the critical accreditation criteria for the attainment of OBE parameters needed for successful completion of NBA/NAAC assessment. The system facilitates capturing of required data in well defined templates, processing of data to generate required analytics for Course outcomes, Program outcomes, Program Education outcomes, graduate attribute attainments critical in OBE measurement process. The entire process in current form is often painful with respect to data collection and processing that is required to generate desired results. The systemic approach developed by EduMeasure through this solution provides much needed support to all the stakeholder involved in NAAC/NBA assessments.
